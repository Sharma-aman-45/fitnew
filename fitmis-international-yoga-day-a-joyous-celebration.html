FitMi’s International Yoga Day: A Joyous Celebration

  • By FitMi Desk
  • On July 05, 2023
  • In International Yoga Day

FitMi celebrated International Yoga Day by hosting special yoga sessions for over 150+ yoga enthusiasts..

The event took place on June 21st, International Yoga Day, at both IIMT University Meerut and the Shivansh Green Resort in Rishikesh.

International Yoga Day

“Yoga is for Everyone” was the event’s theme. IIMT University Meerut organised the event in collaboration with FitMi.

Students took part in a fun activity that included yoga and other activities to encourage healthy living among the students.

An outdoor session was also held at the well-known Shivansh Green Resort in Rishikesh.

Everyone who attended this special session had a great time learning basic poses as well as breathing techniques that will help them relieve stress and tension in their bodies!
